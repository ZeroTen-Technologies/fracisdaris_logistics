Why Choose Fracisdaris Logistic Ltd?

24/7 Security & Climate Control

Your assets are our priority. Our facility is equipped with round-the-clock biometric security, high-definition CCTV, and on-site personnel. Additionally, our precision climate control ensures that sensitive goods—from pharmaceuticals to electronics—are stored at optimal temperatures.

Advanced WMS

We utilize a proprietary Warehouse Management System (WMS) that integrates seamlessly with your existing platforms. This technology minimizes human error, optimizes floor space, and automates order processing to ensure lightning-fast turnaround times.

Strategic Location

Minutes away from major interstate highways and international shipping ports, our hub is perfectly positioned for rapid distribution. Our location drastically reduces "last-mile" delivery times and lowers overall transportation fuel costs for our clients.

Real-Time Inventory Tracking

Never wonder about your stock levels again. Through our client portal, you gain 24/7 access to live data. Track every pallet and parcel from the moment it enters our dock until it reaches the end customer with total transparency.
